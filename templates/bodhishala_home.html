About Bodhi Shala

The Bodhi Shala Lab serves as the foundational AI and High-Performance Computing facility of the Centre of Excellence in Artificial Intelligence. The laboratory houses an NVIDIA DGX-1 AI Supercomputer equipped with 8 NVIDIA Tesla V100 GPUs (32 GB HBM2 each) and 512 GB system memory, providing a shared computational platform for advanced Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning research.

The facility also includes six GPU workstations equipped with NVIDIA RTX 2080 Ti graphics cards, supporting model development, experimentation, training, and deployment activities. Supported by enterprise networking infrastructure, centralized power backup systems, and dedicated research workspaces, the Bodhi Shala Lab enables faculty members, research scholars, and students to undertake computationally intensive AI workloads.

The facility supports research and development activities in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Data Science, and related interdisciplinary domains.
