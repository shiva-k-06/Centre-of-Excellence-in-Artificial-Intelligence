Overview

The TATA Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at NIT Tiruchirappalli is dedicated to advancing research in core areas of AI and ML. Our research spans theoretical foundations and practical applications, with a focus on solving real-world problems in healthcare, agriculture, language technology and smart systems. The centre brings together faculty, research scholars and students to collaborate on cutting-edge projects funded by leading national and international agencies.
